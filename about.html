About Me

Biography

I'm a hobbyist photographer, videography with a love for music and traveling. Embarking on a creative path, my background in mechanical engineering and a master's in computer science have given me a unique analytical edge, yet it's in the artistry of photography and videography that I've discovered my true calling. Exploring the world through my lens since May 2022, I've traversed 27 states and 18,000 miles to capture life's ephemeral beauty. My diverse expertise, from wedding photography to commercial product shoots, is complemented by a mastery of editing software and aerial drone videography. As a storyteller, my aim is to infuse meaning into every frame, crafting visuals that narrate the profound tales of everyday life.
