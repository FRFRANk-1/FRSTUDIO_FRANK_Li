Featured Project

CodClimb

A weather intelligence app for outdoor rock climbers

Full Story →
Swift iOS Open-Meteo API Community Reports AI Scoring

Know before you drive to the crag.

Born from the frustration of driving 2 hours to Rumney, NH only to find wet rock. CodClimb combines real-time weather data with community condition reports to give climbers a 0–100 score for any crag, any hour.

Climbers earn reputation as they report — building a live, human-verified conditions layer that no weather API alone can provide.
